Tip Calculator FAQ

What's a standard tip percentage?
For restaurants, 18-20% is standard for good service, 15% for basic service, and 20-25% for excellent service. Tip percentages may vary by location and culture.
Should I tip on the pre-tax or post-tax amount?
Generally, it's acceptable to tip on either the pre-tax or post-tax amount. Many people tip on the pre-tax amount, but tipping on the total (including tax) is also common and appreciated.
How do I split a bill with different tip preferences?
Calculate the total with the highest tip preference, then have those wanting lower tips pay less. Alternatively, calculate each person's meal cost plus their preferred tip amount.
Is it okay to tip less for poor service?
Yes, tips can reflect service quality. For poor service, 10-15% is acceptable, but consider speaking with management about service issues rather than just reducing the tip.
