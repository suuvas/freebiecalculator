FAQs

What is LTV ratio?
LTV = Loan Amount ÷ Property Value × 100. A $200K loan on a $250K home = 80% LTV. Lower LTV = more equity = better rates.
What LTV is needed to avoid PMI?
80% LTV (20% down/equity) avoids PMI on conventional loans. Once you reach 80% LTV, you can request PMI cancellation from your lender.
What is a good LTV for refinancing?
80% or lower gets the best refinance rates. Above 90% makes refinancing harder. FHA streamline allows up to 97.75% LTV.
How do I calculate home equity?
Equity = Home Value − Loan Balance. A $350K home with a $250K mortgage = $100K equity (71.4% LTV).
