Pace Calculator FAQ

What's the difference between pace and speed?
Pace is time per distance unit (e.g., 8:00/mile), while speed is distance per time unit (e.g., 7.5 mph). Pace is commonly used in running, speed in cycling.
How accurate are the race predictions?
Race predictions are estimates based on your current pace. Actual performance depends on training, weather, course difficulty, and race-day conditions. Use as a starting point for goal setting.
Should I train at my race pace?
Most training should be at an easy pace (conversational). Include some race pace work, but 80% of training should be at an easier effort for optimal improvement.
How do I improve my pace?
Consistent training, including easy runs, tempo runs, intervals, and long runs. Gradually increase mileage and intensity. Rest and nutrition are equally important.
