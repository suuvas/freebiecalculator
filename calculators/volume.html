Volume Calculator FAQ

What is volume?
Volume is the amount of 3D space occupied by an object, measured in cubic units (m³, cm³, ft³, in³).
How do you calculate the volume of a sphere?
Sphere volume = (4/3) × π × radius³. For example, a sphere with radius 3 has volume (4/3) × π × 27 ≈ 113.1 cubic units.
