Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate square footage of a room?
For a rectangle, multiply length × width in feet. A 12 ft × 15 ft room = 180 sq ft. For irregular shapes, divide into rectangles and add each area together.
How many square feet is a 12×12 room?
A 12×12 room is 144 square feet (12 × 12 = 144). This equals about 13.4 square meters.
How do I convert square feet to square meters?
Multiply square feet × 0.0929. Example: 500 sq ft × 0.0929 = 46.45 sq meters. Or divide by 10.764.
How many square feet are in an acre?
One acre = 43,560 square feet. To convert acres to sq ft, multiply by 43,560.
How do I measure an L-shaped room?
Divide the L-shape into two rectangles, calculate each area, and add them. For example: Section A (10×12 = 120 sq ft) + Section B (8×6 = 48 sq ft) = 168 sq ft total.
