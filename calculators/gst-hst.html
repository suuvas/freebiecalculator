FAQs

What is the GST rate in Canada?
Federal GST is 5%. Some provinces combine it with provincial tax into HST (13–15%). Others charge GST separately plus PST or QST.
Which provinces have HST?
Ontario (13%), New Brunswick (15%), Nova Scotia (15%), Newfoundland & Labrador (15%), and PEI (15%) use HST — a combined federal/provincial tax.
Does Alberta have sales tax?
Alberta has no provincial sales tax — only the 5% federal GST. This makes Alberta the lowest-tax province for consumer purchases in Canada.
How do I find the pre-tax price from a total?
Pre-tax = Total ÷ (1 + tax rate). Example: $113 in Ontario (13% HST): $113 ÷ 1.13 = $100 pre-tax, $13 tax.
