Retirement Planning FAQ

How much should I save for retirement?
Financial experts typically recommend saving 10-15% of your income for retirement. However, the exact amount depends on your retirement goals, current age, and expected lifestyle in retirement.
What's a realistic rate of return to expect?
Historically, the stock market has returned about 10% annually before inflation. A conservative estimate of 6-8% after inflation is commonly used for retirement planning.
What if I'm starting late?
Starting late means you'll need to save a higher percentage of your income. Consider maximizing contributions to catch-up contributions in 401(k)s and IRAs if you're over 50.
Should I include Social Security?
This calculator doesn't include Social Security benefits. You can estimate your benefits at ssa.gov and reduce your retirement savings goal accordingly.
