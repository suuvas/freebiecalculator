Hours Calculator FAQ

How is overtime calculated?
Overtime is calculated on any hours worked beyond your set regular hours per day. The default is 8 hours with 1.5x overtime rate, but you can customize both settings.
Can I track breaks and lunch time?
Yes! Enter break time in minutes for each day. The calculator automatically subtracts break time from your total worked hours.
What time formats are supported?
You can view results in decimal format (8.75 hours) or time format (8:45). Input uses standard time picker for accuracy.
Is this suitable for payroll?
Yes! The calculator provides detailed breakdowns suitable for payroll processing, including regular hours, overtime hours, and pay calculations.
