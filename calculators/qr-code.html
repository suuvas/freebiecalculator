QR Code Generator FAQ

What is a QR code?
QR (Quick Response) codes are 2D barcodes that can store various types of information like URLs, text, contact details, or WiFi credentials. They can be scanned by smartphones and other devices.
How do I scan a QR code?
Most smartphones have built-in QR code scanners in their camera apps. Just point your camera at the QR code and follow the prompt that appears.
Are QR codes free to create and use?
Yes, QR codes are free to create and use. There are no licensing fees or restrictions for generating basic QR codes.
What's the difference between PNG and SVG formats?
PNG is a raster image format good for digital use, while SVG is a vector format that scales perfectly for print materials and large displays.
