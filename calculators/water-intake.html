Water Intake FAQ

How much water should I drink daily?
The general recommendation is about 2.7 liters (11 cups) for women and 3.7 liters (15 cups) for men, but this varies based on activity, climate, and individual factors.
Do other beverages count toward water intake?
Yes, all fluids contribute to hydration, including tea, coffee, milk, and water-rich foods. However, plain water is the best choice for optimal hydration.
What are signs of dehydration?
Signs include thirst, dark yellow urine, fatigue, dizziness, headache, and reduced urination. Severe dehydration requires medical attention.
Can you drink too much water?
Yes, drinking excessive amounts can lead to water intoxication or hyponatremia. Stick to recommended amounts unless advised otherwise by a healthcare provider.
