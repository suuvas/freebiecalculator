Understanding Body Fat Percentage

📏 Measurement Tips

Measure in the morning before eating. Keep tape measure level and snug but not tight. Take measurements at the same time of day for consistency.

🎯 Why It Matters

Body fat percentage is more accurate than BMI for assessing health. It distinguishes between muscle mass and fat mass for better fitness tracking.

📊 Interpretation

Essential fat is required for bodily functions. Athletes have lower percentages due to higher muscle mass. Average ranges vary by age and gender.

Body Fat Calculator FAQ

How accurate is the US Navy method?
The US Navy method is generally accurate within 3-4% when measurements are taken correctly. It's more accurate than BMI but less precise than DEXA scans or hydrostatic weighing.
What's a healthy body fat percentage?
For men: 6-24% is considered healthy, with 10-18% being optimal for most. For women: 16-30% is healthy, with 16-25% being optimal. Athletes may have lower percentages.
How often should I measure body fat?
Monthly measurements are sufficient for tracking progress. Daily fluctuations are normal due to hydration, food intake, and measurement variations.
Why do women need higher body fat?
Women require higher essential fat (10-13%) compared to men (2-5%) due to reproductive functions and hormone production. This accounts for the higher healthy ranges.
