FAQs

What are macros?
Macros are protein (4 cal/g), carbohydrates (4 cal/g), and fat (9 cal/g). Tracking them lets you hit specific nutrition targets for your health and fitness goals.
How much protein do I need per day?
General health: 0.8g/kg. Muscle building: 1.6–2.2g/kg (0.7–1g/lb). Weight loss with muscle preservation: 1.8–2.4g/kg.
What macro split is best for weight loss?
40% protein / 30% carbs / 30% fat is popular for fat loss. High protein preserves muscle in a calorie deficit. Some prefer higher carbs (40/40/20) or ketogenic (5/25/70) approaches.
What is TDEE?
TDEE (Total Daily Energy Expenditure) is all calories burned per day including exercise. Your macros are calculated from your TDEE adjusted by your goal (cut, maintain, or bulk).
