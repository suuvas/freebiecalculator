Frequently Asked Questions

How does the love calculator work?
Our love calculator analyzes letter patterns and combinations in both names to produce a fun compatibility score. The result is generated for entertainment purposes using a name-based algorithm.
Is the love calculator accurate?
Love calculators are pure entertainment — not science. Real relationship success comes from communication, shared values, trust, and mutual respect. Names don't determine love!
What does a high love percentage mean?
80–100% means strong name compatibility in our fun system. But remember — plenty of real-life couples with "low" scores are deeply happy together. Take it with a smile!
Can I test celebrity names?
Absolutely! Try any combination — celebrities, fictional characters, friends, or that special someone. The calculator works with any names you enter.
What truly makes two people compatible?
True compatibility comes from shared values, emotional intelligence, communication, mutual respect, similar life goals, and genuine care for each other. No algorithm can measure what makes love truly work!
