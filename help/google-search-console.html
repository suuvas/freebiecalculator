📊 How to Submit to Google Search Console

Submitting your sitemap to Google Search Console tells Google about all your pages and accelerates indexing — often from weeks to days.

🚀 Quick Links

  • Google Search Console →
  • Your Sitemap URL: https://freebiecalculator.com/sitemap.xml
  • Your site now has 66 pages in the sitemap

Step 1: Go to Google Search Console

  1. Visit search.google.com/search-console
  2. Sign in with your Google account
  3. Click "+ Add property"

Step 2: Add Your Property

  1. Choose "URL prefix" option
  2. Enter: https://freebiecalculator.com
  3. Click Continue

Step 3: Verify Ownership

The easiest method for a static site:

  1. Choose "HTML file" verification method
  2. Download the verification file (e.g., google1234abc.html)
  3. Upload it to the root of your website (same folder as index.html)
  4. Click Verify in Search Console

Alternative: Use the HTML meta tag method — copy the <meta name="google-site-verification"> tag and add it to the <head> of your index.html.

Step 4: Submit Your Sitemap

  1. In the left sidebar, click Sitemaps
  2. Enter: sitemap.xml
  3. Click Submit
  4. You'll see "Success" — Google will crawl all 66 pages!

Step 5: Request Indexing for Key Pages

For your highest-priority pages, use the URL Inspection tool to request fast indexing:

  1. Paste a page URL into the search bar at the top
  2. Click "Request Indexing"
  3. Priority pages to submit first:
