Help & Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about using FreebieCalculator.com

General Questions

Is FreebieCalculator.com completely free?

Yes, all our calculators are completely free to use. We support the site through ethical advertising and never charge for calculator usage. There are no hidden fees, subscriptions, or premium features.

Do I need to create an account?

No account registration is required. You can use all calculators immediately without providing any personal information.

Do you store my calculation data?

No, we don't store any of your personal data or calculation inputs. All calculations are performed locally in your browser for maximum privacy. Your data never leaves your device.

Calculator Accuracy & Usage

Are the calculators accurate?

Our calculators use standard mathematical formulas and are regularly reviewed for accuracy. However, results are for informational purposes only and should not replace professional financial, medical, or educational advice.

Can I trust the financial calculator results?

Our financial calculators use industry-standard formulas, but actual loan terms, interest rates, and fees may vary. Always consult with financial professionals for major decisions.

Are health calculator results medically accurate?

Health calculators provide general estimates based on standard formulas. They should not be used for medical diagnosis or treatment decisions. Always consult healthcare professionals for medical advice.

Technical Support

Do the calculators work on mobile devices?

Yes, all our calculators are designed to work perfectly on mobile devices, tablets, and desktop computers with responsive design.

Which browsers are supported?

Our calculators work on all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. We recommend keeping your browser updated for the best experience.

What if a calculator isn't working properly?

Try refreshing the page first. If the problem persists, please contact us with details about your browser and device.

Privacy & Security

How do you protect my privacy?

We follow privacy-by-design principles. No personal data is collected, stored, or transmitted. All calculations happen locally in your browser.

Do you use cookies?

We only use essential cookies for basic functionality like remembering your theme preference (light/dark mode). No tracking cookies are used.

Is the site secure?

Yes, our site uses HTTPS encryption and follows web security best practices to protect your browsing experience.
