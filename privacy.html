Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2024

Last Updated: June 29, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to FreebieCalculator.com ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle information. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and protection of information when you use our website and services.

FreebieCalculator.com is designed with privacy-by-design principles, meaning we've built our platform to minimize data collection and maximize your privacy protection.

Information We Do NOT Collect

To be completely transparent, here's what we do NOT collect:

  • Personal Information: We do not require or collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personal identifiers
  • Calculation Data: We do not store, log, or transmit any numbers, values, or results you enter into our calculators
  • User Accounts: We do not require account creation or user registration
  • Location Data: We do not access or store your geographic location
  • Device Information: We do not collect device-specific information or unique identifiers
  • Browsing History: We do not track your browsing patterns across our site or other websites

How Our Calculators Work

All calculations on FreebieCalculator.com happen entirely within your web browser using JavaScript. This means:

  • Your calculation inputs never leave your device
  • Results are computed locally on your computer
  • No data is transmitted to our servers during calculations
  • You can use our calculators offline once the page loads

Cookies and Local Storage

We use minimal cookies and browser storage for essential website functionality:

Essential Cookies

  • Theme Preference: We store your dark/light mode preference in browser local storage
  • Session Cookies: Temporary cookies for basic site functionality that are deleted when you close your browser

No Tracking Cookies

We do not use cookies for tracking, advertising, or analytics purposes. We do not use third-party tracking cookies or pixels.

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some site functionality (like theme preferences), but all calculators will continue to work normally.

Third-Party Services

Google AdSense (Advertising)

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google AdSense may use cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. You may opt out of personalized advertising at Google Ads Settings or aboutads.info.

All ad slots on our pages are clearly labeled "Advertisement" and are visually separated from calculator content. We do not place ads in ways that interfere with navigation or deceive users.

Learn more: How Google uses information from sites that use our services.

Google Analytics (Usage Statistics)

We may use Google Analytics to collect anonymized, aggregate statistics about how visitors use our site (pages visited, time spent, device type). This helps us improve our calculators and content. No personally identifiable information is collected. You can opt out using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Data Security

Since we don't collect personal data, there's minimal risk to your privacy. However, we still maintain security best practices:

  • HTTPS Encryption: All communication with our website is encrypted
  • Secure Hosting: Our website is hosted on secure, reputable platforms
  • Regular Updates: We keep our website software and security measures up to date
  • No Data Breach Risk: Since we don't store personal data, there's no personal information at risk

Children's Privacy

FreebieCalculator.com is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13. Since we don't collect any personal information, we don't have special procedures for children's data. Parents can feel confident that their children's privacy is protected when using our calculators.

International Users

FreebieCalculator.com is accessible worldwide, and our privacy-first approach means users from all countries receive the same high level of privacy protection. We comply with major privacy regulations including:

  • GDPR (European Union)
  • CCPA (California, USA)
  • PIPEDA (Canada)
  • Other regional privacy laws

Since we don't collect personal data, most privacy regulation requirements are naturally satisfied.

Your Rights

While we don't collect personal data, you still have certain rights:

  • Right to Information: This privacy policy provides complete transparency about our practices
  • Right to Access: You can review all data we have about you (which is none)
  • Right to Deletion: You can clear any local storage (theme preferences) through your browser
  • Right to Portability: Not applicable since we don't store your data
  • Right to Rectification: Not applicable since we don't store personal data

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we make changes:

  • We will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Significant changes will be prominently announced on our website
  • We will never reduce your privacy protections without clear notice

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we're protecting your privacy.

Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@freebiecalculator.com
  • Contact Form: FreebieCalculator.com/contact
  • Response Time: We typically respond to privacy inquiries within 48 hours

We take privacy concerns seriously and will work with you to address any questions or issues promptly.

Summary: FreebieCalculator.com is designed to respect your privacy completely. We don't collect personal data, don't track your usage, and perform all calculations locally in your browser. Your privacy is protected by design, not just by policy.
