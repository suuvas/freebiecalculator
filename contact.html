Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report a bug or error in a calculator?
Please email us at bugs@freebiecalculator.com with details about the issue, including which calculator you were using, the inputs you entered, and what result you expected vs. what you got. Screenshots are also helpful!
Can I suggest a new calculator?
Absolutely! We love hearing ideas for new calculators. Email us with your suggestion, explaining what the calculator should do and how it would be helpful. We review all suggestions and implement the most requested ones.
Do you offer API access or embed codes?
Currently, we don't offer API access or embed codes, but this is something we're considering for the future. Contact us at business@freebiecalculator.com if you have specific integration needs.
Can I use your calculators on my website?
Please link to our calculators rather than copying them. This ensures users get the most up-to-date versions and helps support our free service. Contact us for specific licensing questions.
How do you ensure calculator accuracy?
We use established mathematical formulas and test all calculators extensively. However, calculators are for informational purposes only. For critical decisions, always consult with qualified professionals.
