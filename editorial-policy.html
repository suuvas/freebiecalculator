Editorial Policy

FreebieCalculator.com is committed to providing accurate, reliable, and helpful calculation tools. This editorial policy outlines our standards for content creation, review, and maintenance.

Our Editorial Standards

Accuracy First

  • All calculations are based on established mathematical formulas and industry standards
  • Financial calculators follow formulas from authoritative sources (IRS, Federal Reserve, banking industry)
  • Health calculators align with WHO, CDC, and medical consensus guidelines
  • Educational tools reflect current academic standards and best practices

Source Verification

  • We cite primary sources for all formulas and methodologies
  • Government agencies, academic institutions, and professional organizations are preferred sources
  • We avoid relying on secondary sources or unverified information
  • All sources are reviewed for credibility and currency

Clear Communication

  • Instructions are written in plain language accessible to all users
  • Complex concepts are explained with examples and analogies
  • Results include explanations of what the numbers mean
  • Limitations and assumptions are clearly stated

Content Creation Process

Research Phase

  1. Identify user needs through search trends and feedback
  2. Research authoritative sources for formulas and standards
  3. Analyze existing tools to identify gaps or improvements
  4. Consult with subject matter experts when needed

Development Phase

  1. Code calculations using verified formulas
  2. Test with multiple scenarios and edge cases
  3. Compare results with other reputable calculators
  4. Ensure mobile responsiveness and accessibility

Review Phase

  1. Subject matter expert reviews accuracy
  2. UX specialist evaluates usability
  3. Technical review for performance and compatibility
  4. Final approval before publication

Update and Maintenance

Regular Reviews

  • All calculators are reviewed quarterly for accuracy
  • Tax and regulatory calculators updated when laws change
  • Health calculators reviewed against latest medical guidelines
  • User feedback is monitored and addressed promptly

Version Control

  • Changes are documented with dates and reasons
  • Major updates are noted on individual calculator pages
  • Archive of previous versions maintained for reference
  • Users are notified of significant changes through update log

Conflict of Interest Policy

FreebieCalculator.com maintains editorial independence in all content decisions. We do not accept payment for calculator placement or favorable treatment. Our recommendations are based solely on accuracy, usefulness, and user benefit.

Financial Independence

  • Calculator content is not influenced by advertising partnerships
  • We do not promote specific financial products or services
  • All tools provide neutral, educational information only
  • Revenue comes from display advertising, not affiliate marketing

User Privacy and Data

All calculations are performed in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit your calculation inputs or results. Your privacy is protected by design. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Feedback and Corrections

We welcome feedback on our calculators and content. If you notice an error or have suggestions for improvement, please contact us. We investigate all reports promptly and make corrections when needed.

Correction Process

  1. Verify the reported issue with our team
  2. Research the correct information or formula
  3. Update the calculator and note the change
  4. Respond to the user who reported the issue
